Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chewy, Soft, & Irresistible!

You would never know these are vegan.

These are the BEST vegan chocolate chip cookies!
Easy, soft, chewy, and no weird ingredients- just your typical baking staples.
Bon appetit!

    Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegan butter softened
  • ½ cup white granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons non-dairy milk
  • 1½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ to 1 cup vegan chocolate chips semi-sweet

    Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Prepare a baking sheet by lining it with parchment paper. If I'm using a light-colored, non-stick baking pan I typically do not line it because there is no need, but I do line when using a dark metal baking pan to prevent overbrowning on the bottom.
  2. Cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar using a hand mixer or stand mixer.
  3. Mix in the vanilla extract and non-dairy milk.
  4. Mix in the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Roll the cookie dough into balls (I use a heaping tablespoons worth for each cookie) and place on the baking sheet an inch or two apart.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes. I usually bake for 11 or 12 minutes for a slightly golden brown bottom. If you want super soft cookies, stick to 10 minutes.
  8. Let cool on the baking sheet for at least 20 minutes.

NOTE: makes 10 - 12 cookies.
